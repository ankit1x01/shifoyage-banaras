Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a Shifoyage trip different?

+
We focus on healing, soulful exploration, and inner connection, not just sightseeing. Our trips are designed to be transformative experiences where you connect with yourself, nature, and a community of like-minded people. You travel with the founder, not just a guide, ensuring a deeply personal and nurturing journey.

What is the group vibe like?

+
Our groups are community-driven, safe, and nurturing. We use fun icebreakers and deep, connection-based activities to turn strangers into friends. You're not a customer; you become part of the Shifoyage family. Many of our travelers form long-lasting bonds that continue long after the trip ends.

I'm a solo traveler. Is this trip for me?

+
Absolutely! A majority of our travelers are solo adventurers. Our trips are the perfect environment to meet new people in a safe and welcoming setting. The group activities and shared experiences make it easy to connect with others.

What kind of stays can I expect?

+
We thoughtfully select comfortable and character-filled stays that enhance the journey. We prioritize vibe, safety, and comfort over generic, basic hotels. In Banaras, this means cozy hotels nestled in the heart of the ancient city, allowing for an immersive experience.
